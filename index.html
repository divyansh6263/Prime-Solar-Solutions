PM Surya Ghar Yojana

Government of India Rooftop Solar Subsidy (CFA) Scheme. Get maximum direct subsidy back into your bank account.

Up to 2 kW System

₹30,000 / kW

Direct subsidy of ₹30,000 per kilowatt for system capacities up to 2 kW. Ideal for small-scale residential setups.

  • Max Subsidy: ₹60,000
  • Best for: 1-2 AC / Fan / Lights load

₹78,000 Capped

Direct subsidy capped at a maximum of ₹78,000. Best for larger villas, bungalows, and heavy commercial arrays.

  • Best for: 3-phase high capacity loads

4 Steps to Claim Your Solar Subsidy

1

Apply Online

Register on the PM-Surya Ghar National Portal with your electricity connection details.

2

Feasibility & Install

Get approval from your local DISCOM, and we will install solar panels from the Approved List (ALMM).

3

Net-Meter & Inspect

Your local electricity board will inspect the installation and fit a bi-directional net-meter.

4

Subsidy Credited

Submit connection details and claim request. Subsidy gets directly deposited into your bank account within 30 days!

We are a certified channel partner. We handle all paperwork and applications for you!
